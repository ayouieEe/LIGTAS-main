    SAFETY PROTOCOL

    Fire Safety Plan

    1. Evacuate immediately and stay low to avoid smoke inhalation.

    2. Use a wet cloth to cover your nose and mouth.

    3. Call emergency services (911) and follow marked evacuation routes.

    4. Do not use elevators. Meet at your designated assembly point.

    SAFETY PROTOCOL

    Earthquake Response

    1. Drop, cover, and hold on until shaking stops completely.

    2. Stay away from windows, glass, and heavy furniture.

    3. Move to a safe outdoor area away from buildings.

    4. Check on your household and await further instructions.

    SAFETY PROTOCOL

    Flood Evacuation

    1. Move to higher ground immediately. Avoid floodwater.

    2. Keep your emergency kit and important documents dry.

    3. Follow official evacuation routes from Pasig City LDRRMO.

    4. Do not walk or drive through moving water.
